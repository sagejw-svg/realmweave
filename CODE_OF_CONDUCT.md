# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Realmweave to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of experience, background, or identity. We pledge to make
participation a respectful and constructive experience.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps the community:

- Being kind, respectful, and patient, especially with newcomers.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its players.
- Assuming good faith and disagreeing without hostility.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory comments.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or review.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces (issues, pull requests,
discussions, and any official channels) and when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Reports of unacceptable behavior can be sent privately to the head developer at
**sagejw@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and handled discreetly. The
head developer may take any action deemed appropriate, from a warning to a
permanent ban from project spaces.
